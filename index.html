Welcome!

We are Irish wreck online, our goal is to provide a reliable diver's guide to the ship wrecks around Ireland. Here you will find the resources you need for your next adventure under the sea, whether it be maps of shipwrecks, illustrations of ship types, or coastal grids we've got it all.

Scuba Clubs in Ireland

Whether you are a complete novice or already qualified, there are a number of scuba clubs in Ireland that will make you feel welcomed! These clubs are a great way to meet new people who share your interest in shipwrecks around Ireland. Perhaps, you may even meet your next scuba buddy!

Since club information is constantly changing and being updated rather than trying to maintain the changes on our website we have instead provided links to the organisations main websites below. These are the three main organisations in Northern Ireland and the Republic of Ireland.
